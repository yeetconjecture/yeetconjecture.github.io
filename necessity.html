Necessity

Why do i write here? Right now (on 27.03.26) I am feeling a desperate need to escape. I do not know what i am trying to escape from, all i know is that i need to escape from here. There are many feelings here, fear, loneliness, dissatisfaction, longing for something, confusion etc. I do not know what to do with these feelings. Are feelings something that must be done with? I dont know that either. But they lie within me, telling me something. I do not always listen to them. I distract myself most of the time. But I am afraid that i am stuck, and will be stuck here if i dont feel them, or face them, or understand them, or whatever. I am desperate to escape here. I need to change.

I am tired of having a second hand perspective of the world. Just riding on beliefs about the world, without actually experienceing it, its scary and lonely. But perhaps i am doing exactly that right now. Still, i want this to be a place where i can explore my thoughts and experiences uncontaminated by any ideas, however much that is possible.
